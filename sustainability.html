SUSTAINABLE andGENUINE

What makes our product truly exceptional is the innovative and sustainable technology we employ. In our strictly clean factory, located in the heart of Nida, we use a natural mechanical amber press machine to shape tiny amber particles into captivating forms. It's a meticulous process that involves the pure power of pressing, with no additives whatsoeverâ€”just the pristine essence of water.

The color of our amber is achieved solely through the use of water. The water not only regulates the color but also ensures that each piece retains its natural qualities. This commitment to purity and tradition sets our Black Baltic Sea Amber apart, making it the most precious gem of the Baltic Sea region.

Before entering the production line, this raw material undergoes a stringent certification process at the national assay laboratory, guaranteeing its authenticity and meeting the company's rigorous standards. The commitment to quality extends throughout the manufacturing process, with constant inspections and certifications applied to all process materials. This unwavering dedication to quality control not only ensures that each piece maintains the natural beauty and integrity of amber but also instills confidence in customers, assuring them that their Amberdam accessories are crafted with precision and authenticity at every step.
