      Unique &sustainable gemprocessing

      What makes our product truly exceptional is the innovative and sustainable technology we employ. Using a unique and eco-friendly process, tiny amber particles are meticulously pressed into captivating forms using only the pure power of pressing, without the use of any additional chemicals—just the pristine essence of water. This method not only preserves the integrity of the amber but also ensures that each piece retains its natural qualities. Black Baltic Sea Amber is renowned for its distinctive scent, inherent healing capabilities, and is widely recognized as the most precious gem of the Baltic Sea region.
